Homemade Risotto Recipe

Description

A creamy Italian rice dish cooked with broth until it reaches a rich, velvety consistency.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 300g arborio rice
  • 120ml dry white wine
  • 1 L warm chicken or vegetable stock
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Warm the stock in a saucepan and keep it at a gentle simmer.
  2. Heat the oil and butter in a large pan, then saut√© onion until soft.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the rice and toast for 1-2 minutes until the edges look translucent.
  5. Pour in the wine and stir until absorbed.
  6. Add stock one ladle at a time, stirring often, until absorbed before adding more.
  7. Continue until rice is creamy and al dente, about 18-20 minutes.
  8. Stir in Parmesan, season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
